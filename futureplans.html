Future Plans

My future plans are very complex and will probably get blocked by life but, I plan on going to either IUPUI or IU Bloomington after High School and double majoring in Computer Science and Business. After that I plan on working in Computer Science for a good amount of time and hopefully saving up to begin law school. One of my ambitions is to become an immigration lawyer, but, with the surplus of immigration lawyers there are now. I would most likely be a buisness lawyer, which is my secondary choice. I plan on using my computer science skills and hopefully my business degree to later on open my own law firm and go into private practice as I grow older.
